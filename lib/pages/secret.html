ZEN3MP

----------------

Under construction.

Little information: Micro blogging social network with RPG functionality. Explore dungeons, fight monsters, buy items, battle friends, etc. Post to earn experience and currency. Messaging will be available.

The name is based on this being the third major incarnation of Zeniea (First gen: Bulletin Board/AJAX Chat, Second gen: ACMLM Board/zQuest) and it will be based on community and multiple people, hence the MP. Being a play on of the project TES3MP.

Visit the News Feed for updates on the projects progress.
